    About this item
    Bandar Laddu, also known as Machilipatnam Laddu, is a famous sweet delicacy from the town of Machilipatnam (also called Bandar) in Andhra Pradesh, India. It is a traditional dessert with a unique preparation method and flavor that has been cherished by locals for generations.     Bandar Laddu is known for its crumbly yet soft texture, which melts in the mouth. The rich flavor comes from the slow-roasting of chickpea flour in ghee, which gives it a deep, nutty taste.
