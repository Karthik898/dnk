    About this item
    Bandar Mithai, also known as Machilipatnam Mithai or Bandar Sweet, is a traditional sweet originating from the coastal town of Machilipatnam (Bandar) in Andhra Pradesh, India. Known for its unique texture and taste, Bandar Mithai has become a local specialty and is highly popular across the region.
    Bandar Mithai is primarily made from besan (chickpea flour), sugar, and ghee (clarified butter). Some versions may include a hint of cardamom for added flavor.
